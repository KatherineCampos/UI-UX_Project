CANADA

Canada is a country in the northern part of North America with 10 provinces and three territories. This country has four seasons: spring, summer, fall, and winter.

Canada, the world’s second largest nation with a huge array of sights and activities to offer for travelers. There truly a lot to do in Canada. You can explore national parks, crashing waterfalls, and glacial lakes. You can hike through mountain scenery or visit one of its multicultural cities and enjoy endless foodie tours at the country’s best culinary locations.


Castle Provincial Park

Castle Provincial Park is a provincial park in southern Alberta, Canada. Castle Provincial Park offers great camping and wild mountain adventures on paths less travelled. It has four rustic campgrounds, two group camping areas, comfort camping, and nine free-of-charge designated camping areas. You can also paddle across Beaver Mine Lake or you can dive-in to its popular cliff diving spot at Castle Falls.

Royal Tyrell Museum

The Royal Tyrrell Museum is a Canadian tourist attraction and a centre of palaeontological research known for its collection of more than 130,000 fossils. Visit this Dino museum with your children and learn about the different kinds of dinosaurs.

Mt. Norquay

Mt. Norquay is a mountain and ski resort in Banff National Park, Canada that lies directly northwest of the Town of Banff. Mt.Norquay is one of three major ski resorts located in the Banff National Park. The regular ski season starts early December and ends mid-April.
