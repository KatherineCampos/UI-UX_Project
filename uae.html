UNITED ARAB EMIRATES

The United Arab Emirates (UAE) is in Western Asia. It is called the United Arab Emirates because it is a federation of hereditary absolute monarchies which consists of seven states or called emirs - Abu Dhabi (the capital emirs), Ajman, Fujairah, Sharjah, Dubai, Ras Al Khaimah, and Umm al-Qaiwain. UAE has two distinct weather, Summer and Winter. Summer starts at the end of April and ends on the first week of October. If you say summer in UAE, it is certainly summer when temperature raises more than 45 degree Celcius but do not worry, you can still visit this country during this hot weather because it has the biggest malls like the Dubai Mall, where you can chill and shop. Winter season is my favorite when I can feel a cool mild wind with right temperature that is about 16 degree Celcius temperature.

Dubai, the city where I lived in, is an independent city-state is filled with magnificent tourist destinations. So, plan your destination in one of the places in Dubai and other emirs of UAE and discover the wide array of ancient and modern sites.


Burj Al Arab and Burj Khalifa

            Before exploring the country, book and stay with one of these two luxury hotels in Dubai and live like a sheikh or sheikha for a day or two. Have a cup of tea while enjoying a sky view from your sophisticated room or simply hang out with other travelers from their highest lounge and restaurant.

Old Dubai Culture

            Discover the Old Quarter of Dubai and forget that you are in an ultra-modern city by stepping back in time. Start your tour at the oldest city’s port and learn the history and culture of Dubai then head to the three different museums and then shop from the three souqs -- shop for souvenirs from the Old Souq, different authentic spices from Spice Souq, or real gold jewelry from Gold Souq.

Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque

            Explore the capital city of UAE, Abu Dhabi, and visit the fourth largest mosque in the world, Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque and be amazed by its splendid architecture. Because you are visiting a mosque, be sure to wear more conservative dress code such as “abaya” for women and “thawb” for men.

Dubai Miracle Garden

            Smell the fresh scents from different kinds of flowers from Dubai Miracle Garden and be fascinated with the landscapes and floral sculptures forming shapes like Emirates Airbus, Mickey Mouse, Big Teddy Bear, etc. There are other activities you can do here such as watching flower parade and live shows. But make sure to visit during winter because it is closed during the summer.
